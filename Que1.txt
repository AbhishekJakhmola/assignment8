Time tuple is the way in which python handles the time and date .
Python handles time tuple as 9 numbers as shown:

SR no	Field		Values
____________________________________________________
  0	Year		1 to 9999
  1	Month  		1 to 12
  2	Day		1 to 31
  3	Hour		0 to 23
  4	Minute   		0 to 59
  5	Second		0 to 60 or 61 as leap second
  6	Day of the week	0 to 6
  7	Day of the year	1 to 366
  8	Daylight savings	-1,0,1
